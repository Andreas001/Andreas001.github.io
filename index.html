Edge's Dream

A visual novel game.

This is a university project. “The Edge’s Dream” is a visual novel centering around the one-year life of a campus student forced to take a study he does not feel attached to. Having to deal with the situation he is in, he soon mostly fails in his studies until a certain hope appears before him, allowing him to realize his passion for art and design while still maintaining his current path as an Informatics student.

The player will decide on the character’s daily activities and make choices which will eventually lead to a conclusion for the main character’s final path as an UX designer

  • Made with: Renpy
  • Category: Story focused, Visual Novel
